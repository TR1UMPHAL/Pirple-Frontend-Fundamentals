Veggie Cheese Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 ready-made pizza base
  • 1.5 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 pinch powdered black pepper
  • 80 gm shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 teaspoon powdered salt
  • 50 gm chopped onion
  • 30 gm chopped capsicum (green pepper)
  • 50 gm chopped tomato
  • 20 gm sliced mushroom

Steps to Prepare:

  1. Preheat the oven on convection mode at 250 degree Celsius.
  2. Spread the tomato sauce evenly on the pizza base.
  3. Take a chopping board and chop an onion, a tomato, a capsicum and a mushroom on it.
  4. Mix all the chopped vegetables and your favorite seasonings in a bowl.
  5. Microwave these vegetables in a microwave safe bowl for around 30-40 seconds.
  6. Spread this vegetable topping on each pizza base.
  7. Sprinkle grated mozzarella cheese at the top of the pizza.
  8. Bake at 250 degree Celsius till the cheese melts, for around 10-12 minutes.
  9. Cut the delicious Cheese Pizza into slices and serve with sauces.
